# Wiki as Knowledge Base

## The Wiki-First Approach

In an agentic system, the wiki is not just documentation — it is the
primary interface between humans and agents. Agents read the wiki
through RAG to understand context, decisions, and procedures.

## What Goes in the Wiki

| Content Type | Example | Why |
|-------------|---------|-----|
| Architecture decisions | ADR-001 | Agents understand design choices |
| Operational guides | How to deploy | Agents follow procedures |
| Standards | Naming conventions | Agents enforce consistency |
| Runbooks | Server rebuild | Disaster recovery |

## Wiki Quality = Agent Quality

If the wiki is messy, agents give messy answers. Invest in frontmatter,
consistent naming, regular cleanup, and fresh re-indexing.
